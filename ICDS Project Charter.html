SMART Project Charter

For ICDS - Delivering Excellence in Refurbishment and Fit-Out Projects

Project Title

Refurbishment and Fit-Out Excellence for Commercial and Hospitality Sectors

Objective

To complete high-quality refurbishment and fit-out projects ranging from €1m-4m within the Dublin and North Kildare region, ensuring 95% client satisfaction, adherence to budget, and delivery within agreed timelines.

Scope

- Refurbishment and fit-out projects for hotels, commercial spaces, and extensions.
- Projects valued between €1m-4m.
- Locations: Dublin and North Kildare.
- Includes stakeholder management, health and safety compliance, and sustainability practices.

Challenges

- Managing tight deadlines and ensuring on-time delivery.
- Controlling costs to avoid budget overruns.
- Addressing supply chain disruptions for materials.
- Ensuring compliance with health and safety regulations.
- Maintaining high-quality standards across all projects.

Key Deliverables

- Completion of all projects within the agreed timeline (90% of projects delivered within 6 months).
- Budget adherence with a maximum deviation of 5%.
- Implementation of a robust quality assurance process.
- Client satisfaction rate of 95% or higher.
- Compliance with all health and safety regulations.

Stakeholders

- Clients: Hotel owners, commercial property managers.
- Internal Teams: Project managers, site managers, and labor teams.
- Subcontractors: Specialized trades for fit-out and refurbishment.
- Suppliers: Material and equipment providers.
- Regulatory Bodies: Health and safety inspectors, environmental agencies.

Timeline

- Project Kickoff: Within 2 weeks of contract signing.
- Milestone 1: Completion of initial design and planning phase within 1 month.
- Milestone 2: 50% project completion within 3 months.
- Final Delivery: 100% project completion within 6 months.

Success Metrics

- 90% of projects delivered on time.
- Budget deviation limited to 5%.
- Client satisfaction survey results of 95% or higher.
- Zero major health and safety incidents.
- Positive feedback from stakeholders on quality and communication.
